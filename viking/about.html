Some information about us and what we do!


We are Safe Travels Viking Festivals. The term "Safe Travels" comes from the same Norse rune our logo shares. We host events where all guests are invited to come dressed as our favorite Northern warriors, where there are plenty of games, food, drinks, and festivites to take part in! A welcoming community awaits you with plenty of oppurtinuty to make a name for yourself and become legend in the shared mythos.

While it may be encouraged by our talented actors and staff, guests are under no obligation to act within character, and are free to walk around the event unencumbered of role-play. However, the largest draw our guests often tell of us is the open, vibrant, and diverse community and cast of characters that make up the event.

The dates, locations, and times on the home page are for meet ups in partnering spaces throughout Florida. While we do run our own events, they are annually, so if you wish to catch our community on a more frequent basis, meet up with us at one of our partnering events!
